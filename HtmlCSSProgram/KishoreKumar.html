Kishore Kumar

Kishore Kumar was an Indian playback singer and actor. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest and most dynamic singers in the history of Indian music.He was one of the most popular singers in the Indian film industry, notable for his yodeling and ability to sing songs in different voices.
