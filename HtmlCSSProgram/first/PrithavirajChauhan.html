Prithaviraj Chauhan

Prithviraja III, popularly known as Prithviraj Chauhan or Rai Pithora, was a king from the Chauhan dynasty who ruled the territory of Sapadalaksha, with his capital at Ajmer in present-day Rajasthan. Wikipedia
Born: 1149, Gujarat
Died: 1192, Ajmer
Children: Jhoda, Govindaraja IV, Bhirda, Shaikha, Taikha, Laikha
Structures: Taragarh Fort - Ajmer
Parents: Someshvara, Karpuradevi
Siblings: Pratha Chauhan, Hari Raj Chauhan, Govind Tai
Grandparent: Arnoraja
